Why is the data collected?

To satisfy safety audit requirements of the Ground Control Station.

To provide traceability of Commander actions.

To support incident investigation.

To comply with professional software engineering standards.


How is it secured?

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) restricts access to logs to “Auditor” roles only.

Logs are stored securely on the server.

No public API endpoint exposes audit logs.

Passwords/API keys are never stored in plain text.
